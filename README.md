Resonance is a web-based AI tool for communication training. It supports people who want a guided way to prepare for important discussions, with an emphasis on building clarity, confidence, and practical effectiveness.

The core of the experience is conversation practice driven by AI. That lets users rehearse, think back on the interaction, and reinforce better communication patterns in a structured environment. It is meant for repeated training and skill-building, not just one-time tips.
